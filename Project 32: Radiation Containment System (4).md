Project 32) Radiation Containment System (4) 💀☢️ (2025)
After the 12 days war between Israel and Iran I decided to find a way to prevent the radiation spreading, and since all the nuclear sights have gone and the radioactive materials buried deep underground.

1) RadioActive vacuum cleaner:
At first I thought how to throw the materials to sun using the solar armors of mine, but then I realized that it takes too long to take them to the sun's atmosphere, but Deepseek helped me to how to remove the radiation out of the surface, 
turns out that radiations can get out of the surface using instance heat, but that only send to air and not to destroy, 
so I decided to create a vacuum cleaner which uses heater at the head of the cleaner and a radioactive sealed box to keep the radioation and sealed, 
Unfortunately it's not totally make things easy, because it can't go everywhere, the radiation might also be in water and pips in the houses, walls and etc;

2) Radioactive hunter microbots:
After the first design I was wondering why can't we just use nanobots and microbots to do this job, by creating a cloud full of the microbots, powered by swarm AI that can detect and seal the readion and radioactive materials, 
I thought it would be possible but some of the materials are too big and the cloud can only take small amount of the them, so I decided to think bigger;

3) RadioActive chamber (Decon Prison):
After the second test I realized that I should design a chamber that use instance heat to remove the radiation from the surface this chamber is full covered by lead Which not allow the radiation to get to spread further, 
but then I realized that someone or something must move those, so I used cloud microbots and vacuum cleaner design,
The microbots send the materials and radiation to the sealed chambers that installed on the chamber's body, and they will be delivered to outside the chamber, without any damage inside or out, 
and for the Drones and microbot's I must add Contamination chamber... Still a work in progress;

5) radioactive particle absorber (for Robots) (2026):
following this, I managed to find a better solution to absorb radioactive particals, A multi-agent radioactive decontamination system combining thermal surface vaporization, microbot-guided swarm coordination, and mobile lead-lined containment chambers, inspired by Deus Ex's Implanted Redbreather augment!, an Implanted Redbreather that was originally designed to help users to breath through places where Toxic spread across small areas, but by changing its design we are able to add this augment to a Robot to absorb radioactive particals and breath clean oxygen out, by using the Robot's body part to create instant heat to vapraize surface particales, we maybe able to improve this system to gather these materials and even make places like chernobyl free from radioactive disasters;
