Project 26) Operation God of war (8) 
(2024)
These projects are mystical weapons of God of war video games, I found similarities between their abilities and the modern technology,
(P.S one of them is already introduced: Forst axe, Operation Frost Zero)

1) Forst Axe: (already mentioned in Operation Frost Zero!) 
Originally, this design has Been taken by God of war video games, this axe has a mini sized cryogenic reactor installed at the bottom of the axe, as the axe's handle, 
(or we can add it inside the axe's blade, but it would be a risk and it may unstable the reactor)
It's incredibly powerful and can create huge damages over objects and live targets, but I create it to shot down wildfires and controled froze lands during the time that fire spreads, regardless of the place's space status;

2) Draupnir Spear:
I made a similar design of kratos's Draupnir Spear, by using Nanotech 3d printing, the user can use multiple fake versions of the spear at the same time and keep the original for combat if necessary, 
The challenge is the power source for the nanotech energy cost for duplication;

3) Guardian Shield:
This adptive shield can be installed on the user's bracers and users are able customize it in different sizes (light and heavy), it can be thrown like other shield regardless of the user's body strength and for the material, 
we can use Carbon nanotube-reinforced alloy for its durability and lightweight, P. S (Each shield's design can have a unique effect);

4) the Blade of Olympus:
The greatest weapon of all time especially in God of war game, this blade has been forged by the Olympian gods and it has the energy similar to plasma which is visible on the blade's edge,
